Mobile Games
Caramel Tech's key expertise is developing next-gen high performing Free to Play (F2P) mobile games.

Working closely with Kabam Inc. on a RPG game, we are experienced in achieving the top line metrics through both game design and Live Ops, and the team is a strong believer in the Games as a Service (GaaS) model.

Moreover, honing in our experience working on ports of high quality and mega hit mobile games like Fruit Ninja, Jetpack Joyride and the Stick Sports series, we understand both the nuances of developing a fun, sticky and highly polished game from scratch, as well as creating products that have high LTVs and rest on a strong business model.

Our original IP Blades of Battle RPG boasts extremely high soft-launch ARPDAU figures and compelling retention rates. We are big believers in building player communities. Our community managers and support teams are passionate about engaging with the players (weekly events, design, new features, etc.), and quickly resolving any issues. This has led to a growth of both, a loyal player base and revenue. We operate games extremely efficiently, hence driving a (very) high ROI.
