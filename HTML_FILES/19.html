Internet Trends Report
Dec 4, 2012




Mary Meeker, a partner at Kleiner Perkins Caulfield and Byers a top silicon valley based Venture Capital firm, has just published a super insightful and stunning report on the internet trends in 2012. The report is titled "2012 Internet Trends Year-End Update".


Key highlights from the report:
- 2.4 billion internet users; YoY growth @ 8%.
- 1.1 billion smartphone subscribers worldwide i.e. ~17% of the world smartphone market.
- 29% of adults in the U.S. now own either a tablet or an e-reader.
- Mobile devices now account for 13% of worldwide Internet traffic, up from 4% in 2010.
- Mobile app and advertising revenue has grown at an annual rate of 129% since 2008, and now tops $19 billion.
- iPods changed the media industry, iPhones ramped even faster and iPad growth (3x iPhone) leaves "siblings" in dust.
- Android is to Mobile now what previously Windows was to PC.


For a full report, please visit: 

http://www.kpcb.com/insights/2012-internet-trends-update
