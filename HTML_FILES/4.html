ABOUT US!
 
Caramel Tech Studios is a premier game development studio which came into existence over a warm cup of Caramelatte in the wintry start of 2011. The young and energetic management is laser focused on attracting and hiring only the best talent. Our team mainly consists of industry experts who have experience working with top tier game studios like Kabam Inc., Halfbrick Studios and others. Therefore, quality is and always will be our top priority!

Quality though is not our only concern. With the additional benefit of having an offshore studio in Asia, companies can tap into top talent - which understands games - across the globe. We can also ramp up our team in a short span; our partner studios thus do not need to worry about the ever-changing market or their requirements.

Our driving goal is to make the next-gen top performing mobile games.

ADVISORS:
 

Caramel Tech Studios is run and operated by gaming industry veterans, focusing on high quality mobile games. The team has worked on block buster games like Blades of Battle RPG, Fruit Ninja, Jetpack Joyride, and many others. The studio has a history of partnering with top tier studios like Electronic Arts (EA), Kabam, Big Fish Studios, Scopely and others.

Apart from designing and launching games, the team prides itself on running profitable live operations and customer support for new and existing games while building strong in-game communities.

He was the co-founder of Karma Science, an innovative mobile commerce platform acquired by Facebook in May 2012. Previously, Ben was the co-founder of Tapjoy, a platform offering mobile publishers solutions for application discovery, rich advertising and virtual good sales. He has also worked at Microsoft as an engineer on the original Xbox Live team. Ben has an undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering and an MBA both from the University of Michigan.
